Cybersecurity Tips and Tricks

  • Always use strong, unique passwords for each of your accounts.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
  • Be cautious of phishing emails and messages. Verify the sender before clicking on any links.
  • Keep your software and devices updated to protect against vulnerabilities.
  • Use a reputable antivirus program and keep it updated.
  • Regularly back up your important data to a secure location.
  • Be aware of common scams and how to avoid them.
  • Do not share your MFA codes or passwords with anyone.
  • Monitor your accounts regularly for any suspicious activity.
  • Educate yourself about the latest cybersecurity threats and best practices.
